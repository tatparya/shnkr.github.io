Who is SHNKR?


Producer and DJ based in NYC, SHNKR is a creative force in House and Techno, shaping a bold new direction for the genre. Infusing his work with influences from his Indian roots, SHNKR weaves traditional rhythms with modern, hypnotic beats, creating a captivating sonic tapestry that is both culturally rich and innovatively fresh.

One year into the making, SHNKR has already left an indelible mark by headlining sold-out shows at esteemed venues in New York and Boston and garnering over 100k streams across platforms and over 1000 monthly listeners on Spotify. His signature Indo house and techno sets seamlessly blend authenticity with a vibrant energy that enchants audiences.

SHNKR's edits and remixes have garnered support from over 1000 DJs worldwide, affirming the universal appeal of his sound. With an arsenal of unreleased music poised to captivate the industry, SHNKR is a rising talent set to resonate across the global music scene.


Location: New York City

Headlined a sold-out show at Bijou, Boston's premier club known for hosting techno and house legends like Argy, Cassian, Yotto, Blonish, Maz, and Francis Mercier. Captivated an audience of 400+ with a signature Indo house and techno set.
